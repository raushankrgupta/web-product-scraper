Frequently asked

Is my photo safe?

Your photos are uploaded over HTTPS and stored encrypted in our cloud. We never share or sell your data. You can delete your account and all associated photos from inside the app at any time.

Is it really free?

Yes. The free tier includes 5 try-ons per day. The Plus plan raises that to 50/day and removes the watermark. The Pro plan is unlimited. You can also use the app once without even creating an account.

Which stores does it support?

Myntra, Flipkart, Amazon India, TataCliq and Peter England today. More retailers ship every month — request yours via support@tryonfusion.com.

How accurate is the fit?

The AI uses your full-body photo plus optional body measurements (height, weight, chest, waist, hips) to render a realistic try-on. Accuracy goes up sharply once you add measurements.

Can I try on as a couple?

Yes — couple try-on is one of our headline features. Add two profiles, pick the outfits, and we generate a coordinated look in one tap.

Does it work for ethnic wear (sarees, kurtas, lehengas)?

Yes. Ethnic categories actually perform especially well thanks to the themed try-on packs (wedding, festival, casual).

Will there be an iOS app?

Coming. Until then, use the Android app or the Chrome extension on any product page.

How do I delete my data?

From the app: Profile → Delete Account. We immediately soft-delete your account and wipe associated photos. Or email support@tryonfusion.com and we'll do it for you.
